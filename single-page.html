Our Story
Real-Time Communications was born out of the need for specialized fiber optic cable services. Gathering experience and knowledge of the industry for over twenty years combined, while making strong and valuable connections with our customers has helped us to find a market that needed a provider and we are now here to answer the call. We enjoy our work and pride ourselves on our ability to help troubleshoot complex problems quickly by providing answers and information to our clients.
